Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have an eye exam?
It’s recommended to see your dentist every 6 months for a routine check-up and cleaning, unless advised otherwise.
What should I do in an eye emergency?
Experiencing sudden vision loss, eye injury, redness, or severe pain? Call our clinic immediately — we offer same-day emergency eye care to protect your vision and comfort.
Do you offer services for kids?
Absolutely! We provide gentle, friendly pediatric eye care for children of all ages — ensuring healthy vision and a comfortable experience at every visit
What are my options for vision correction?
We offer glasses, contact lenses, and surgical options — tailored to your vision needs and lifestyle preferences
Is LASIK surgery safe?
Yes, LASIK surgery is considered safe and effective when performed by a qualified eye surgeon. Most patients experience improved vision with minimal risks and a quick recovery time.
